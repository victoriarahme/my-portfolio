About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! My name's Victoria. I'm a 26 year old computer programming student at Humber Polytechnic. Ever since I was a teen, I've been very curious about technology. I used to love playing around with html on Tumblr, and spent most of my time obsessed with computers. I graduated with my first diploma in hospitality in 2019 and have been in the work force ever since. After coming to terms with my feelings of unfulfillment in my career, I became interested in the challenge and problem-solving aspect of Computer programming. This curiosity is what pushed me to go back to school. I've always been a creative person, and I have dreams of pursuing a career in front-end development or UX design.
