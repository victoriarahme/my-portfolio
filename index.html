About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! My name's Victoria. I'm currently a computer programming student at Humber Polytechnic. Ever since I was a teen, I've been very passionate about technology. I used to love playing around with html on Tumblr, and spent most of my time obsessed with computers. I graduated with my first diploma in hospitality in 2019 and have worked within the industry ever since. After spending quite a few years in the administrative side of things, I started to feel like something was missing. The challenge and problem-solving aspect of Computer programming really appealed to me. This curiosity is what pushed me to go back to school. I love any type of work that's engaging and can keep me on my toes - and I think I've found just that! I have dreams of pursuing a career in front-end development or UX design, and hope to apply my attention to detail to make beautiful web pages.
